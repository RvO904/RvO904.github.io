Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05-01-2026

CoopBanking for Board Games (“the App”) is developed and operated by VoR Dev (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Overview

CoopBanking for Board Games is a local multiplayer companion app designed to help players manage virtual in-game currency during board games. The App does not involve real money transactions and does not require user accounts or online registration.

Any user profile (such as username, avatar, or preferences) is created and stored locally on the user’s device and is not linked to any online account.

2. Information We Process

We aim to minimize data processing. However, the App may handle the following information:

a. Local Network Information

  • The App accesses your local IP address to enable multiplayer functionality within the same network.
  • The App may request permission to detect nearby devices in order to create and join game rooms.
  • This information is used solely for local connectivity and is not transmitted to our servers.

b. Locally Stored User Data

All user data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. This includes:

  • Username
  • Avatar
  • Color preferences
  • Game data (transactions, messages, player interactions)
  • App settings and preferences

Game room data is hosted on the device of the player acting as the host.

c. No Server-Side Data Storage

We do not collect, store, or process any user data on external servers. All information generated or used by the App remains exclusively on users’ devices or within the local network environment during active sessions. At no point is personal or gameplay data transmitted to, stored in, or accessible from any remote server infrastructure controlled by us.

3. Data Sharing Between Users

Because the App enables local multiplayer:

  • Your username, avatar, and in-game activity may be visible to other players within the same game room.
  • This data is shared directly between devices over the local network.

Please note:

  • Other users may retain this information locally on their devices as part of saved game data.
  • We do not control or have access to data stored on other users’ devices.
  • We do not collect, store or have access to any personal data ourselves. However, third-party services such as AdMob may collect data independently.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage data

This data is processed by Google in accordance with its own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google’s privacy policy for more details.

We do not use any other third-party analytics or tracking tools.

5. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using appropriate storage mechanisms.

We do not operate any remote servers and do not store user data externally.

However, data shared with other players during gameplay may persist on their devices.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the App, or
  • Clearing the App’s data through your device settings

This will permanently remove all data stored on your device.

Please note: Data shared with other players may still exist on their devices and cannot be deleted by us.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13, or under the age defined by applicable data protection laws in their country.

Since the App does not collect or store personal data on external servers and all data remains locally on the user’s device, the potential risks associated with children’s data are significantly limited.

Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise children’s use of the App.

8. International Users and Legal Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights under applicable data protection laws. Since the App does not store data on remote servers, you can exercise your right to deletion at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its data.
