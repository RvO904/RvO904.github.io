Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05-01-2026

ChordIt (“the App”) is developed and operated by VoR Dev (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Overview

ChordIt is a guitar chord finder and learning tool that allows users to explore chord shapes, identify chords, and create custom setlists for practice and organization.

The App is designed to work primarily offline. No user accounts or registration are required, and we do not collect or store personal information on external servers.

2. Information We Store

All data created by the App is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • App configuration settings
  • Custom setlists created by the user
  • Saved chords within setlists

This information remains on your device and is never uploaded or synchronized to any server.

3. Audio Content

ChordIt may include preloaded audio files used for chord playback and learning purposes. These audio files are stored locally with the application and do not require internet access.

4. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob and its partners may automatically collect certain data, including:

  • Device information (such as model and operating system)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App usage data related to ads

This information is processed by Google in accordance with its own privacy policies. We do not control or have access to this data.

You can learn more by reviewing Google’s privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

All application data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers, and no data is transmitted to or stored in external databases controlled by us.

6. Data Deletion

You may delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the App, or
  • Clearing the App’s data from your device settings

This will permanently remove all locally stored information, including setlists and settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

ChordIt is an educational application intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users of any age, including children under 13.

Since all data remains local and no accounts are required, no personal data is transmitted to us.

8. Internet Usage

The App does not require an internet connection to function. However, an internet connection is required for loading advertisements served by Google AdMob.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
